Motivation Counter

Replace your new tab with a real-time counter. Track your age, countdown to a date, or to the end of the year.

⏱

Age counter

Watch your age tick up in real time, down to the millisecond. A quiet reminder that time is passing.

📅

Countdown modes

Count down to the end of the year or to any date you choose — a deadline, a birthday, an event.

💬

Daily quote

A new motivational quote every day from a curated collection. Toggle it off if you prefer silence.

🔒

Completely private

No tracking, no analytics, no external connections. All data stays in your browser.
