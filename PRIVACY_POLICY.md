# Privacy Policy for Motivation Counter Extension

**Last updated: 14/04/2026**

## Data Collection
**This extension does not collect any user data.**

## Information Storage
- Your birth date and display preferences are stored locally in your browser only
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics, tracking, or monitoring is performed

## Data Usage
Your birth date is used solely to calculate and display your counter. This data never leaves your device.
